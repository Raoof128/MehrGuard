# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment
We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free project environment.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful in technical discussions.
- Provide constructive, actionable feedback.
- Assume good intent while holding high engineering standards.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Doxxing or publication of private information.
- Repeated disruptive conduct in issues or pull requests.

## Reporting
Report conduct issues to project maintainers through private channels listed in `SECURITY.md`.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or restrict participation for violations.
